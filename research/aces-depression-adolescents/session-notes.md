## Key Insights

### Research Foundations
- Field has matured significantly since Felitti et al. (1998) original ACE Study
- Strong dose-response relationship: 4+ ACEs = 4x higher depression risk
- Recent systematic reviews (2023-2024) provide robust evidence base

### Theoretical Evolution
- Movement from **Cumulative Risk Model** (simple ACE scoring)
- To **Dimensional Model (DMAP)** - threat vs. deprivation pathways
- Recognition of timing, severity, and context in adversity impact

### Critical Findings
- **Protective Factors Matter:** 3-5 Positive Childhood Experiences = 50% lower depression odds
- **Health Equity Critical:** Structural racism and social determinants must be addressed
- **Adolescent-Specific Gaps:** Less research on proximal adolescent impacts vs. adult outcomes
