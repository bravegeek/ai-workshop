# ACEs and Depression in Adolescent Populations

## Project Overview

This research project explores the connection between Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACEs) and depression in adolescent populations, with a focus on both academic research and public health applications.

## Research Focus

- **Primary Goal:** Literature review on ACEs-depression relationships in adolescents
- **Perspective:** Research/Academic + Public Health/Policy
- **Expert Role:** Public Health Researcher specializing in ACEs and Mental Health Epidemiology

## Project Structure

- `landscape-analysis.md` - Comprehensive initial landscape analysis identifying key papers, frameworks, and researchers
- `literature-review/` - (Future) Systematic literature review materials
