# Cosmos and Chaos - Brainstorming Session

## Game Concept

**Name:** Cosmos and Chaos

**Genre:** Idle Clicker Game

**Core Mechanic:** Player defends a starbase positioned in the center of the screen from incoming asteroids

## Brainstorming Session Status

**Current Phase:** Context gathering and focus area selection

- **B. Technical Architecture** - Game engine, implementation, platform
- **C. Monetization & Business Model** - Revenue, retention, economy
- **D. Narrative & Theme Development** - Lore, story, world-building
- **E. Production & Development Planning** - Roadmap, MVP, timeline
- **F. Player Experience & Psychology** - Reward loops, difficulty curves, retention

The game starts with a simple premise: defend your starbase from asteroids. This provides an intuitive entry point for players while allowing for expansion into more complex idle/incremental mechanics.
